Smartphone Finder
HEADER
Logo (top-left) → “Smartphone Finder”

Progress Bar (top-right) → Steps 1–6 with percentage or dots.

Step 1: Choose Your Platform
UI Layout:

Large card buttons with icons:

iOS (Apple logo)

Android (Android robot logo)

No preference (mix icon)

Next button (disabled until selection is made)

UX Notes:

When “iOS” is chosen → Show note: “iPhones start from around $500.”

When “Android” is chosen → No price limitation.

Step 2: Choose Brand
UI Layout:

Grid of brand logo cards: Apple, Samsung, Xiaomi, Google, OnePlus, Oppo, Vivo, etc.

Skip option (for no brand preference)

UX Notes:

If “iOS” was picked → Show only Apple.

Click on card → highlight selection.

Step 3: Set Budget Range
UI Layout:

Range slider (min–max price)

If iOS: min starts at $500

If Android: min starts at $100

Show currency selector (optional).

Extra:

Small “Popular ranges” chips: $100–$300, $300–$500, $500–$1000, $1000+ (click to auto-fill slider)

UI Layout:

Icon-based cards:

Everyday use 📱

Work productivity 💼

Social media / Content creation 🎥

Drag-and-drop list:

Camera quality

Battery life

Display quality
